## 2. Judge Q&A Prep

**Q: Why use AI for this instead of just templates?**
**A:** Templates are static. AI understands the *nuance* of the topic. It can bridge the gap between a technical developer's insight and a Gen-Z student's interest in a way a template never could. It adapts context, not just structure.

**Q: What makes this unique compared to ChatGPT?**
**A:** Contextual workflow. ChatGPT requires complex prompting. Our app removes the friction with a specialized interface for platform/tone/audience settings and adds a layer of 'Engagement Scoring' that specifically critiques content for social media success.

**Q: How do you handle scalability?**
**A:** The backend is built on Node.js/Express, making it lightweight. We use stateless API calls to Gemini/OpenAI. For production, we would implement user authentication and a database (like MongoDB) to save history and learn from 'Brand Voice' memory.

**Q: Any ethical considerations?**
**A:** AI-generated content should always be reviewed by humans to ensure accuracy and authenticity. We encourage 'Co-Pilot' usage—AI generates the drafts, but the creator provides the final soul and fact-checking.
